Our Projects


UrbanNest

UrbanNest

UrbanNest aims to help buyers and investors identify the most suitable new housing developments in Melbourne. We compile information directly from the developer on all aspects of each development to make sure buyers have access to all the information possible to make a well- informed purchase.

UrbanCrib

UrbanCrib

UrbanCrib is our co-working space project based in Melbourne. The idea is to nurture and create a network of well-equipped and fairly priced co-working spaces in locations suitable for startups and small businesses to make their journey from early-stage startup to making an impact on the community easier.

UrbanCrowd

UrbanCrowd

UrbanCrowd is still in alpha right now. It is a platform for crowd-sourcing quality real estate investments in Melbourne. It aims to allow small private investors to access quality investments otherwise reserved only for the larger institutional investors.
